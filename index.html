01

Research Snapshot

5 gaps identified

The Five Gaps

  • No VSL on the sales page. Text-heavy sales page at the $3K-$5K price point. In 2026, high-ticket buyers expect to hear from the founder before purchasing. A 12-15 minute VSL stacking Shopify endorsement, 14K students, and the domestic supplier mechanism would compress the sales cycle.
  • Retreat model caps scalability. One annual event with finite capacity. If the retreat is the primary differentiator between Premium and Ultimate, scaling Ultimate requires multiple events per year or a digital substitute.
  • No mid-tier between free content and $3K course. 251K combined social following (IG + YouTube) jumps straight to a $3,000 course with no lead magnet bridge. A free blueprint would build the email list and warm the audience.
  • YouTube audience not funneled to email. 102K subscribers consuming free content with no visible lead magnet CTA in video descriptions or end screens. Algorithm-dependent re-engagement.
  • No post-purchase upsell from Premium to Ultimate. $3K buyers have no structured upgrade path to the $5K package with retreat ticket and DFY store.

Highest-Leverage Fix

Build a VSL for the sales page. Create a free lead magnet to bridge YouTube/IG to email. Build a 10-email nurture from download to enrollment. Design a Premium-to-Ultimate upsell sequence.
